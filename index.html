About Me

Lydia, founder & dog sitter at PawPal, is pictured with Sohm to the right. Sohm is one of many past PawPal Buddies she's cared for over the years.

Hi! My name is Lydia and though I was raised in rural Northwestern Wisconsin, I currently serve the Hudson Valley, Westchester, New York and Danbury, Connecticut area. I've had a love of animals as far back as I can remember. I wasn't able to have dogs growing up and would be so excited to hang out with my friends who were dog parents. Eventually, this developed into me housesitting for them while they were away. Overtime, my business quickly spread by word of mouth. I have been officially running this business for three years although I have many more years of experience than that.

I spent over a year working closely with a vet at a pet hospital, which has provided me with valuable insight. Because I don't have any pets or children of my own, my extremely flexible schedule let's me treat your dogs like the family they are to you, with the upmost care. My past experience has lent me the oppertunity to care for dogs of all ages, sizes, and diet needs for different timeframes. My services include activites such as administering pills/topical medications, hair brushing/baths, walks, play and plenty of snuggles. You'll be kept updated with pictures often so you can have a carefree time because we both know you won't be able to stop thinking about your furbaby while you're gone. Although, my niche is dog sitting, I can also cater to other pets. Feel free to reach out to me with any questions you might have; I usually respond within the hour. Your pets and I will have so much fun together!
