About Me

*Lydia, founder & dog sitter at PawPal, is pictured with Sohm to the right. Sohm is one of many past PawPal Buddies she's cared for over the years.

Hi! My name is Lydia* and though I was raised in rural Northwestern Wisconsin, I currently serve the Hudson Valley, Westchester, New York and Danbury, Connecticut area. I've had a love of animals as far back as I can remember. I wasn't able to have dogs growing up and would be so excited to hang out with my friends who were dog parents. Eventually, this developed into me housesitting for them while they were away. Overtime, my business quickly spread by word of mouth. I have been officially running this business for three years although I have many more years of experience than that.

I spent over a year working closely with a vet at a pet hospital, which has provided me with valuable insight. Because I don't have any pets or children of my own, my extremely flexible schedule let's me treat your dogs like the family they are to you, with the upmost care. My past experience has lent me the oppertunity to care for dogs of all ages, sizes, and diet needs for different timeframes. My services include activites such as administering pills/topical medications, hair brushing/baths, walks, play and plenty of snuggles. You'll be kept updated with pictures often so you can have a carefree time because we both know you won't be able to stop thinking about your furbaby while you're gone. Although, my niche is dog sitting, I can also cater to other pets. Feel free to reach out to me with any questions you might have; I usually respond within the hour. Your pets and I will have so much fun together!

Book a Service

Now for the serious business stuff...

Payments are due the first day of the stay or drop in visit. I accept CashApp, Venmo, and Paypal. You may also leave behind a check or cash at your home.

New Client Requirements:

Each new client must make sure their furbabies are up-to-date on rabies' shots and schedule a Meet N Greet a minimum of 24 hours before the stay to make sure we're a great fit. During this visit I would love to know more about you and your furbaby. Things like:

What is your pet's schedule or routine?

What vaccines does your pet have?

Does your pet need any medication?

What allergies does your pet have?

How does your pet interact with other dogs or people?

What is your pet's favorite toy?

Are they house trained or crate trained?

Does your pet have a favorite spot in the yard?

Drop in visits

Drop in visits includes a 15 to 30 minute walk/playtime and feeding/watering and costs $30 per visit

Stays

Unfortunately, I do not offer boarding services at this time. All stays are done overnight at the client's home. Stays are tailored to your furbabies needs and normal scheduled routine. This involves regular feeding, watering, walks, playtime etc. I can also pick up mail, water plants, and bring out trash to the end of the driveway at the client's request.

Stays cost $60 a night with additional pets being a $10 add-on.

Furbabies under 6 months old start at $70 a night with additional pets being a $10 add-on.

Holiday rates:

Holiday weekend stays are $75 a night, Friday through Monday, and resume normal pricing for the rest of the stay.

Holidays during the week are $75 for the night of the holiday and resume normal pricing for the rest of the stay.

Drop in visits on a holiday or over a holiday weekend are $40 a visit.
